1 The Beckman Coulter Interlaboratory Quality Assurance Program (IQAP) is a peer comparison program offered to users of Beckman Coulter instruments worldwide. IQAP complements and enhances the laboratory’s in-house quality control. Customers with supported instruments configured with Beckman Coulter reagents and using Beckman Coulter calibrators and controls are eligible to participate in this program. IQAP allows users of Hematology and Life Sciences (LS) to obtain peer group comparison reports of their laboratory’s quality control data. IQAP is a software only project. IQAP has no access to any patient data or other data that constitutes PHI or ePHI. IQAP is not used for patient care. Therefore, IQAP does not classify as a medical device or software.
